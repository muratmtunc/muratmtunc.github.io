Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Information Systems at Jindal School of Management, University of Texas at Dallas. My committee chairs are Srinivasan Raghunathan and Huseyin Cavusoglu.

I am broadly interested in economic modeling and data-driven decision making with a focus on applications in managing digital platform's information strategy in competitive online marketplaces.

Methodologically, I am interested in (i) game theory modeling, (ii) econometrics, (iii) machine learning, and (iv) online experiments.

I am on the academic job market for 2019-20.
